Policy & Governance 08
How We Govern

01
Transparency

Financial statements, board resolutions, and fund usage are reported clearly and made available to the congregation on request.

02
Accountability

Every leader, including the Presiding Bishop, is accountable to the Board. No one is above the governance framework of this church.

03
Independence

The Board includes independent members who provide objective oversight without conflict of interest, ensuring decisions serve the mission.

04
Stewardship

Every shilling given to God through this church is treated as a sacred trust — designated funds ring-fenced, audited annually, reported honestly.
