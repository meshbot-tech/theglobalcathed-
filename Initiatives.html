01
Initiative One

Church Planting

Establishing Spirit-filled congregations in new territories across Kenya and beyond — bringing the Gospel to communities that have yet to experience the transforming love of Christ. Each planted church becomes a centre of worship, discipleship, and community impact.

Active Projects

Project Rabbits

Church Planting — Phase A

A grassroots church-planting initiative targeting underserved rural and peri-urban communities. Project Rabbits focuses on rapid multiplication of small, culturally rooted congregations — intimate gatherings that grow organically into fully established local churches through discipleship and community engagement.

Active

Project Rhino

Church Planting — Phase B

A bold, strategic church-planting effort targeting larger population centres and regional hubs. Project Rhino deploys seasoned church planters with full structural and financial support to establish self-sustaining congregations with capacity for wide regional influence, leadership training, and further multiplication.

02
Initiative Two

Leadership Development

The Church rises or falls on the quality of its leaders. Global Cathedral is committed to identifying, training, and releasing a generation of Kingdom leaders who carry both character and competence — equipped to lead in the church, marketplace, family, and public life.

Our Approach

Raising Leaders for the Nations

Through structured mentorship, theological grounding, and hands-on deployment, Global Cathedral equips leaders for every sphere of life. Our development pathway moves people from congregation to character to calling — producing leaders who are both spiritually deep and practically skilled.

Explore Ministries ›
I
Identify & Disciple Recognising gifts and potential in every member, then creating intentional discipleship pathways that develop Christlike character as the foundation of all leadership.
II
Train & Equip Structured leadership schools, mentorship cohorts, ministry internships, and practical training programmes covering theology, administration, communication, and vision.
III
Deploy & Release Releasing trained leaders into their God-given assignments — in local churches, businesses, schools, government, and communities — with ongoing accountability and support.
03
Initiative Three

Humanitarian Projects

Compassion is not optional for the Church — it is central to the Gospel. Global Cathedral addresses real, tangible needs in communities through targeted humanitarian projects that restore dignity, alleviate suffering, and demonstrate the love of Christ in practical, measurable ways.

Active Projects

Food Programme

Humanitarian — Nutrition & Food Security

A systematic food relief and nutrition programme targeting vulnerable families, children, and the elderly in underserved communities around Nairobi and across Kenya. The programme combines emergency food distribution with sustainable food security education, kitchen gardens, and community feeding initiatives.

Active

Clean Water Provision

Humanitarian — Water, Sanitation & Health

Access to clean water is a fundamental human right and a prerequisite for healthy, dignified life. This project drills boreholes, installs water purification systems, and establishes sustainable community water points in areas where communities face severe water scarcity — transforming health outcomes and reducing disease.
